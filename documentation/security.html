Modify the current language - LMS

  • Created : 27 September 16

To update the words of the language follow the steps.


* Go to System Settings.

* Now click on Translate located at the top

* Here you can see the list of compatible languages, then click the Update button according to your language.



Congratulations! You have learned to change the language correctly.
