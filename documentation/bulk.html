Bulk Invoice - LMS

  • Created : 27 September 16

To create bulk invoices you must perform the following steps:


* Go to Accounting in the navigation menu.


* Now click on the Payments in the top menu and click on the New Payment button.

Now fill the all form fields and click on Create Invoice. Ready, now the students will receive a notification in your account.
